Something about me

Hello everybody! I created the "My first penguin" website. This is my first website and I chose this topic because I liked so much the game "Club Penguin" when I was a child. I created this website because I am grateful for those memories and now I wanna give you a penguin-friend! The first penguin you will encounter is Bipo. He is a penguin cub who will tell you a few things about him and his family. I wish you a pleasant reading and welcome to our family!
