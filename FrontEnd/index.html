Something about us

Hello everybody! We have created the "My First Penguin" website. This is our first website, and we chose this topic because we loved the game "Club Penguin" so much when we were children. We created this website to express our gratitude for those memories, and now we want to introduce you to a penguin friend! The first penguin you'll meet is Bipo. He is a penguin cub who will share a few things about himself and his family. We hope you enjoy reading and welcome to our family!
